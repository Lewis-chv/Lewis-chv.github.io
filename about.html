About the Artist

Irma Chavez, a passionate artist and loving nurse known for her passsion of creating abstract art. She specializes in abstraction of natural landscapes/themes and aims to inspire different takes on timeless concepts.

Her unique take on the artistic portrayal of nature is one of a kind.

Biography

Irma Chavez was born in Durango, Mexico in 1945. She studied at the University of Texas at Brownsville and became a licensed vocational nurse.

Throughout her career, Irma has exhibited her passion in various fields. Her hobby of painting is a longstanding one, conveing complex emotions through the stroke of a brush.

Achievements

  • Awarded recognition by Valley Baptist Medical Center throughout her years of nursing.
  • Featured artwork in many residential properties throughout South Padre Island and Brownsville.
  • Excellent communication, completed many successful commissions for artwork with satisfied clients.
